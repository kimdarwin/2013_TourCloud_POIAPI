(2) Everyone paid for a round of drinks except Tom. I'm not surprised.
That's Tom all over. He never give anything away.

(3) To show up somewhere uninvited or unexpectdly.
초대받지 않은 곳에 별안간 나타나다.
(4) To sleep // to stay at s.o elses house for the night.

누군가의 집에서 하룻밤 신세지기. 일시적숙소나 하룻밤 피할곳을찿음.

(1) My computer crashed again.
내 컴퓨터가 또 멈췄네.
(2)My car crashed into a brick wall.
내차가 담벼락을 들이받았어.
(3)The dishes crashed to the floor.

but they brought booze, so we let them stay.

== to be in trouble / to be in hot water.
== suggest a difficult or problematic situation.

(1) He was in a terrible fix. He had behaved wrongly and
he had involved another person in his wrong behavior.
그는 곤경에 처했다.그는 옳지않게 행동을 했을뿐 아니라 
다른 사람을 그의 그릇된 행동에 끌여 들였다.

(2) Please lend me some money. I'm in a terrible fix.
 I need to pay off some creditors.
제발 돈좀 빌려 주게나. 곤경에 빠졌다네. 채권자들에게 돈을 갚아야돼..

경탄,놀람,당황,얼떨떨함을 강조할때..

(1) How an earth could he have got up on to the roof without a ladder?
도대체 어떻게..? 그가 사다리도없이 지붕꼭대기에 올라갈수있었을까?

도대체왜..? 우리가 이런 악천후에 밖에 나가야 하는가..
(3) Who in the world would call at that hour.
도대체누가..? 이런 시간에 방문 한단말인가.
